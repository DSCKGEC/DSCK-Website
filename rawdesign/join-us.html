About us
What we do at DSC KGEC?

DSC KGEC is a student led club, built on the power of communities. We believe that great things happen when young inquisitive minds come together, to explore new technologies and create stuff. It is also a way for students to bridge the industry-academia gap through peer-based, project focused learning.

How do we make technologies fun to learn?

Developers Student Club KGEC is inspired by the Google Developers' Family. The motive is to create a local ecosystem of Developers in and around the Campus. And having fun doing it.
